- time: Postgres SQL timestamp.
- latitude: double. Latitude of the station. Units: degrees_north. Standard Name: latitude. Valid Range: -90.0 to 90.0. Fill Value: 99999.0.
- longitude: double. Longitude of the station. Units: degrees_east. Standard Name: longitude. Valid Range: -180.0 to 180.0. Fill Value: 99999.0.
- nitrate: float. Nitrate.  This is one of the BGC(Bio Geo Chemical) factor. Units: micromole/kg. Standard Name: moles_of_nitrate_per_unit_mass_in_sea_water. Fill Value: 99999.0.
- pres: float. Pressure below sea level. Can be considered as depth in meters.
